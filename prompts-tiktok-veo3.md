## PROMPT 1 — "Gérez votre réputation en 1 clic"

```
A cinematic VERTICAL STORY format (9:16 portrait orientation, like TikTok/Instagram Stories), 4 seconds, split into 3 fast-paced scenes with smooth transitions.

French voiceover (male, confident, punchy tone, fast rhythm): "Chaque avis compte. Chaque réponse aussi. L'IA de Repward transforme vos avis clients en machine à croissance. Un clic. C'est tout. Téléchargez Repward — c'est gratuit."

Scene 1 (0-1.3s): Close-up of a restaurant owner's hands holding an iPhone, the screen shows a dark sleek dashboard app with glowing purple accent colors displaying a 4.8 star rating and review analytics charts. Warm ambient restaurant lighting in the background, shallow depth of field.

Scene 2 (1.3-2.6s): The phone screen transitions to show an AI-generated response to a Google review, with a glowing "Publier" (Publish) button. The owner's thumb taps the button confidently. A subtle purple pulse animation radiates from the button.

Scene 3 (2.6-4s): Pull back to show the smiling restaurant owner behind their counter, phone in hand, with happy customers dining in the blurred background. A floating 5-star rating graphic appears beside them with a subtle glow effect. Modern, premium feel.

Style: Cinematic, dark UI with purple/violet accents, warm restaurant ambiance, professional and modern. No text overlays. MUST be vertical 9:16 portrait format.
```

## Notes techniques pour Veo 3 :
- **Format** : TOUJOURS préciser "VERTICAL STORY format (9:16 portrait orientation)" — c'est critique
- **Durée** : Veo 3 supporte 4s nativement
- **Style** : Le mot "cinematic" améliore la qualité
- **Voix off** : Intégrée dans le prompt — Veo 3 peut générer la voix si supporté, sinon enregistrer via ElevenLabs et superposer
- **Pas de texte** : Ajouter les textes/captions en post-prod sur CapCut/TikTok
- **Musique** : Ajouter un beat tendance TikTok en post-prod
